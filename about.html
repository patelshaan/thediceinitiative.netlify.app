About DICE

The DICE Initiative is powered by MIT LaunchX and based at College Park High School. Since our founding in 2017, we’ve evolved from a startup competition incubator to a thriving service-based entrepreneurship club.

[Photo of early DICE event or logo]

Originally, DICE prepared teams for the Diamond Challenge, encouraging hypothetical business proposals and competitive pitching. But when the COVID-19 pandemic hit, it changed how our student leaders saw the community around them.

Instead of competing with imaginary businesses, our members began solving real problems for real organizations. That spirit of service has carried DICE into a new mission: to help students apply entrepreneurial skills while uplifting the Greater Houston area’s small businesses and nonprofits.

Our Purpose

DICE gives students a purpose. Every year, our members join teams that work directly with local clients — nonprofits, small businesses, or social entrepreneurs. Whether they’re helping develop websites, run social media campaigns, or improve operations, students gain first-hand experience and soft skills.

Our teams are led by experienced peers — category leads — who help members navigate both technical and interpersonal aspects of client work. Everything is student-led, from the project management to the design choices.

Looking Ahead

With dozens of projects completed since 2020, we’re focused on scaling our impact while preserving the tight-knit collaboration that sets DICE apart. We aim to expand our reach, welcome new partners, and help our members discover the joy of creating meaningful change.
